Mark messages to you that are part of a group message using a special symbol
Patch is available for version: alpine 2.00 alpine 2.01 alpine 2.10 alpine 2.11 alpine 2.20 alpine 2.21
 
Alpine marks messages sent to you using the special '+' symbol, which is normally the first character of the index message. This symbol is used regardless of if you are the only receipient of the message, or if your address is part of a group message where you are just one of the receipients. This patch makes Alpine use the '.' symbol for messages where your address is part of the To: field, but you are not the only recipient of the message, and reserves the use of '+' to messages where you are the only recipient.
